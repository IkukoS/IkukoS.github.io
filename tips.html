How to not hating cooking.

  1. Find somebody you can share you meal.
  2. Use online recipes to use your ingredient
  3. Be creative.
  4. Have emargency meals.

1. Find somebody you can share you meal.

When you cook just for yourself. You probably make something to stop your hunger. Cooking should not be overcome your hunger. It should be make everybody happy included you!! Having meals with your family is great idea. Even each one of the family membes has different schedule, try to have family dinner. If you are away from your family, whay don't you invit your friends for lunch or dinner. Their comment about your meal will keep your motivation high. You has to be a pretty good cook though. Do not worry I will give you some secret for members.

2. Use online recipes to use your ingredient

Don't get stress what you are going to make tonight! Check your refrigerator. Find one ingredient you would like to get rid of. Then just go to allrecipes. You can find hundreds recipes with the ingredient!

3. Be creative.

The beautiful cake on the home page was super cool among my daugther's friends. If you have already have skill making cake. Only the thing you need is some googling skill and imagination!! Then you can be a super mom!

4. Have emargency meals.

If you can not cook sometime, that is fine. Your friend called you to go to movie? Your children are hungry and waiting for dinner? Do not get stressed! Have TV dinner ready. Have fun tonight and make home madefood tomorrow! It is not bib deal at all!!
